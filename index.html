ABOUT SHEL CONSTRUCTION LTD

Shel construction was made to contribute to the construction industry through planning, designing, surveying, constructing,managing, maintening and replacing public and private infrastructure. We deal in:

  • General construction of houses, hospitals, offices, play parks etc.
  • Manage ,repair, maintenance,and replacement of public and private infrastructure.
  • Perform or oversee surveying operations to establish building locations, site layouts, reference points, grades, and elevations to guide construction.
  • plan and design transportation systems, hydraulic systems, and structures in line with industry and government standard.
